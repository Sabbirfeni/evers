    • About Us

    Everstech is an organization founded in 2016 in the state of Gujarat, India. Our company focuses on R&D. We also provide technical services & solutions to specific industries. We collaborate with teams of scientists, entrepreneurs and channel partners that work together to build and deploy cutting up edge solutions throughout various industries. We are currently engaged in the fields of Smart Agriculture, Renewable Energy, Electric Mobility, Furniture and specialty coating systems. As the whole world is moving toward an EV transport system, INDIA is for sure not getting behind in the race. INDIA is not leading the way to be the largest 2-wheeler EV consumer along the second most used electric public vehicle nation. With the introduction of many EV in INDIA there is a dire need to manage the charging stations, charging operations, power distribution & consumption along with the user interaction with charging stations. We at EVERS are working for India’s first fully integrated digital EV charging network system. We provide services for electric vehicle users, charge point owners, charge point network operators & power distribution companies. We provide a system to offer charging as a service & analyze the collected data for the prediction of future power consumption, peak demand cycles & consumer behaviour cycle thus in return, predicts & provide the power demand , demand location, demand duration & value during the demand duration.

    The main aim of this initiative is to provide a hassle free & real on time charge point location & charging service for the user, while on other sides providing the complete analysis of the charging point to its owner & suggesting the power demand, duration & tarrif to operate in most efficient manner. Power distribution companies on other hand can match the power demand as per the past consumption cycles & future predictions.
